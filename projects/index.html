Research on the Application of Artificial Intelligence in Lung Cancer Diagnosis through Analysis of Chest CT, Bronchoscopy, and Histopathological Images
Completed
08/2022 – 11/2025
Sponsor: Ministry of Science and Technology, Hanoi, Vietnam
Role: Research Project Member
A national research project funded by the Ministry of Science and Technology, focusing on developing AI-based systems for lung cancer diagnosis. The project involved analysis of multiple medical imaging modalities including chest CT scans, bronchoscopy images, and histopathological images to assist in early detection and diagnosis of lung cancer.
